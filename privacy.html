Introduction

Welcome to Being Technical Writer! This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit my website. By accessing and using this website, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

I am committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about my data practices. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact me.

Information I Collect

Personal Information

I do not collect personal information directly unless you voluntarily provide it. You may choose to share information such as:

  • Name and email address when using the contact form
  • Email address when subscribing to the newsletter
  • Comments and feedback you leave on articles

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit my website, certain information may be collected automatically through third-party services:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring website or search terms

This information helps me understand how visitors use the site and improve the content and user experience.

How I Use Your Information

I use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To improve website content and user experience
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • To respond to your inquiries and messages
  • To send newsletters (only if you subscribed)
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To maintain website security and prevent abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

I do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

My website uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics — Website analytics and traffic analysis (Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdSense — Display advertising (Privacy Policy)
  • Disqus — Comment system (Privacy Policy)
  • GitHub Pages — Website hosting and content delivery
  • Buy Me a Coffee — Voluntary support platform
  • Social Media Platforms — Sharing buttons (Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.)

Each of these services has their own privacy policies. I encourage you to review them to understand how they handle your data.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

While I do not set cookies directly, third-party services used on this website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies:

  • Analytics Cookies — Google Analytics uses cookies to track visitor behavior
  • Advertising Cookies — Google AdSense uses cookies to display personalized ads
  • Social Media Cookies — Social sharing buttons may set cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, this may affect your experience on some websites.

Tip: You can opt out of Google's personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

AI-Generated Content Disclosure

In the spirit of transparency, I want to disclose that some content on this website may be created with the assistance of AI tools. This includes:

  • Content drafting and editing assistance
  • Code examples and technical demonstrations
  • Image generation for illustrations
  • Research summaries and content organization

All AI-assisted content is reviewed, edited, and verified by me before publication to ensure accuracy and quality. The use of AI tools helps me create more content while maintaining high standards.

If you have concerns about any content on this site, please contact me directly.

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data:

General Rights

  • Access — Request a copy of the data I have about you
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your personal data
  • Unsubscribe — Opt out of newsletters at any time

For EU Visitors (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

For California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. Note: I do not sell personal information.

Data Security & Retention

I take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • This website is served over HTTPS (secure connection)
  • Hosted on GitHub Pages with enterprise-grade security
  • No sensitive data is stored on the website servers
  • Third-party services are chosen based on their security practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While I strive to protect your information, I cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

I retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. Analytics data is retained according to Google's data retention policies. Newsletter subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe.

External Links

My website contains links to external websites. I am not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. I encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in my practices or for legal reasons. When I make changes, I will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

I encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how I protect your information.
